Where Do I Start?

Ready to begin your FPV journey? The single best investment you can make is a quality radio transmitter paired with flight simulator practice. Here's why: FPV flying has a steep learning curve, and learning in a simulator costs you nothing‚Äîliterally crash as many times as you want risk-free. Once you've mastered the basics virtually, you'll progress faster in the air and avoid expensive crashes.

Pro tip: The FPV industry has standardized on ExpressLRS (ELRS) as the go-to radio protocol. Make sure whatever radio you choose supports ELRS to ensure compatibility with the latest and greatest drones.
